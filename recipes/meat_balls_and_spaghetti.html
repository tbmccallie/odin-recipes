Meat Balls and Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti with meatballs and a red sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare sauce first. Saute in 3 tbsp. Wesson oil.
    • 2 medium chopped onions
    • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  2. Add:
    • 2 (#2 cans) tomatoes, mashed with fork
    • 1 tsp. oregano
    • 2 tsp. salt
    • 2 cans tomato sauce
    • 1 cup water
    • 2 tsp. parsley
    • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  3. Simmer over low heat one hour. When nearly done, add 1 large can mushrooms (drained). When sauce is almost done, mix the following mixture and shape into 1 inch balls:
    • 1 lb. ground beef
    • 1 tbsp. minced parsley
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • 1/8 tsp. pepper
    • 1/4 lb. ground pork or sausage
    • 2 onions, minced
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1 1/2 tsp. salt
  4. Mix thoroughly. Drop into sauce mixture and cook until done.
